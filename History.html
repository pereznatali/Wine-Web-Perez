History of wines

Vitis Vinifera is the scientific name of the plant that bears the fruit of the grape,

food and raw material for the manufacture of wine.


It's belived to be native to Southeast Asia and Central Southeast Europe.


The grape is one of the most nutritious fruits and rich in Vitamin C.

For a quality wine it is necessary to start from ripe and healthy grapes.

That is, grapes with the right balance between sugars and acidity.


The process for making wine from obtaining the grapes will depend on the wine we want to make.

It can be: white wine, red wine or rose wine.
