How to taste

For the art of tasting a wine, we need calm and concentration,

that will allow us to experience all its nuances and enjoy it in it's complexity.

The analysis of a wine is donde through the senses.

    View
    We use it to observe the color, appearance, texture. It allows us to obtain information
    about the graduation, age, origin, and possible flavor.
    Smell
    We use it to understand inhale and exhale aromas.
    Taste
    We use it to appreciate the nuances of the wine, whether it is sweet, acid, bitter, etc.
    Touch
    We use the inner walls of the mouth and the tongue to perceive the volume of the wine,
    its astringency, temperature, texture and effervescence.
    Hear
    There are those who use this sense to capture the fine sound of wine through its bubbles.
