Red wine

Its main characteristic and what differentiates it from other wines is the selected red grape with which it is produced:


Brancellao, Cabernet Sauvignon, Garnacha, Malbec, Mencía, Merlot, Nebbiolo, Pinot Noir, Sangoviese, Tempranillo, Caladoc, Caiño Tinto.


In terms of color, red wines are characterized by presenting various shades that range from a dark red to a more purplish tone.


Its aroma leads us to think of strawberries, raspberries, cassis, blackberries, cherries, truffles. It also highlights the presence of scents such as vanilla and cinnamon.


The taste of red wine is characterized by intense and deep notes. Tends to bitter and smoky flavors.


The pairing of red wine are cheeses, red meats, fish and pasta.
