Rose wine

Its main characteristic and what differentiates it from other wines are the selected red and white grapes with which it is produced:


Cariñena, Tempranillo, Pinot Noir, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Tempranillo and Garnacha Tinta.


In terms of color, rosé wines are characterized by various shades that range from violet pink, soft, or even with orange flashes.


Its aroma leads us to think of fruits such as raspberries, strawberries, blackberries, grapefruit with herbaceous notes.


The flavor of rosé wine is characterized by being delicate, soft, and fruity.


The rosé wine pairing can be with salads, desserts, cheeses, some red meats, fish.
