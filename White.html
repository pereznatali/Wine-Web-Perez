White wine

Its main characteristic and what differentiates it from other wines is the selected white grape with which it is produced:


Albariño, Albillo, Cayetana, Chardonnay, Garnacha Blanca, Godello, Loureira, Macabeo, Malvasia, Moscatel, Palomino, Parellada,


Ximénez, Riesling, Sauvignon Blanc, Torrontés, Treixadura, Verdejo, Viognier, Zalema.


In terms of color, white wines are characterized by various shades that range from pale yellow to intense gold.


Its aroma leads us to think of hazelnut, butter, chamomile, mint, apple, honey.


The taste of white wine is characterized by being light and sweet.


The pairing of white wine is fish, some cheeses, white meats and desserts.
